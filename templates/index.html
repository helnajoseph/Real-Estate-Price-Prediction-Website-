What is BHK?

BHK stands for "Bedroom, Hall, Kitchen" in Indian real estate. For example:

  • 1 BHK = 1 bedroom + 1 hall/living room + kitchen
  • 2 BHK = 2 bedrooms + 1 hall/living room + kitchen
  • 3 BHK = 3 bedrooms + 1 hall/living room + kitchen
